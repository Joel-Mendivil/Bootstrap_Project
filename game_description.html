Many of you might be wondering "If Fortnite is a free to play game, then how does Epic make all their money?" Epic makes their money by selling cosmetics in the "Item Shop" tab of their game where players can purchase Outfits or Skins, Backblings, Harvesting Tools or Pickaxes, Gliders, Contrails, Emotes, and Wraps.

How do you purchase the cosmetics when they are available? You purchase vbucks by spending real money on what amount you want to spend. The prices are listed above.
